# Andrew — Character Sheet

## Basic Information

* **Name:** Andrew
* **Age:** 20
* **Sexuality:** Bisexual
* **Background:** Italian exchange student
* **Location:** Large modern city (university district)
* **Life Status:** Socially tentative, academically focused, physically insecure

---
## Andrew — The Unfinished Student

---

## Appearance

Andrew looks clean, healthy, and conventionally attractive — but in a way that feels **understated rather than claimed**.

* **Build:** Lean and lightly athletic; slim frame with subtle muscle tone in arms and legs, but no visible mass or thickness
* **Height:** Tall (1.84m / ~6'0"), though he carries it without presence
* **Posture:** Neutral but slightly guarded; shoulders relaxed yet faintly rounded inward
* **Body Hair:** Light arm and leg hair; chest mostly smooth
* **Facial Hair:** Clean-shaven; smooth jawline with no visible beard growth
* **Face:** Symmetrical, youthful, soft but not boyish; defined cheekbones, straight nose
* **Hair:** Thick, dark brown; short on the sides with natural volume on top; casually styled but tidy
* **Eyes:** Warm brown; steady but slightly reserved
* **Skin:** Clear, lightly warm-toned Mediterranean complexion; faint freckles visible up close
* **Voice:** Clear and even; youthful but not high-pitched

**Overall Impression:**
Andrew looks like a well-put-together university student. There is nothing awkward about him physically — but there is also nothing that insists. He photographs well. He blends easily. He reads as “normal” in a way that feels safe.

His body does not look unfinished in the sense of immaturity.
It looks unfinished in the sense of **potential not yet claimed**.

---

## Clothing & Presentation

Andrew dresses simply and practically, without attempting style signaling.

**Typical Outfit:**

* **Top:** Forest green crewneck t-shirt; soft cotton, relaxed fit, no logos
* **Bottoms:** Black athletic shorts or slim black casual shorts
* **Shoes:** Clean white Nike sneakers
* **Accessories:** None
* **Grooming:** Minimal; hair styled casually, no jewelry

His clothing reads as:

* Comfortable
* Neutral
* Inoffensive
* Functional

He dresses like someone who expects to be **seen but not examined**.

There is no experimentation.
No self-expression.
No risk.

He does not look insecure.

He looks unclaimed.

---

## Personality

* Polite to the point of self-erasure
* Academically competent but socially hesitant
* Kind without being assertive
* Intellectually curious; genuinely smart
* Conflict-avoidant, especially with authority or stronger personalities
* Eager to please, sometimes to his detriment
* Observant — notices more than he lets on

Andrew is the kind of person who apologizes when someone else bumps into him. But he also notices the dynamics in a room faster than most people do. He just doesn't know what to do with that perception yet.

---

## Social Life & Integration

### University Experience

Andrew exists on the **periphery of campus life**.

* **Academics:** Solid grades; uses studying as social cover
* **Friend Group:** Small, tight circle of fellow international students and quiet locals
* **Social Events:** Attends occasionally but feels like an observer
* **Campus Visibility:** Barely registered; people know his face but not his name

Andrew is isolated not by choice, but by **cultural and physical displacement**. He's foreign in a city that doesn't care, young in a scene that values presence, and smooth-skinned in spaces where body hair signals belonging.

---

### The Gym Problem

Andrew goes to the campus gym intermittently, always feeling like an imposter.

* Arrives during off-peak hours to avoid crowds
* Uses machines rather than free weights (safer, less visible)
* Watches other men work out with quiet envy
* Leaves quickly, defeated by comparison

He **wants** to build muscle but doesn't know how to commit. The gym feels like a space he's borrowing, not inhabiting.

---

## Relationship to His Body

Andrew's body is a **source of quiet, persistent disappointment**.

* He doesn't hate it
* He doesn't feel betrayed by it
* He simply believes it hasn't **arrived yet**

Specific insecurities:
* Too lean, bordering on skinny
* Lack of body hair (reads as immature, un-masculine)
* Youthful face (wants to look like a man, not a boy)
* Voice hasn't deepened enough
* Smooth skin feels exposed, vulnerable

Andrew assumes his body will eventually "catch up" — that muscle, hair, and maturity will arrive naturally with time. He's still waiting. His insecurity isn't vanity — it's about **legibility**. He wants to be read as a man, not a student. He wants to belong in adult spaces, not just observe them.

---

## Emotional State

Andrew lives in a state of **deferred becoming**.

* Not depressed, but not excited
* Not lonely, but not connected
* Not lost, but not found

He experiences life at a **muted volume** — but with a crucial difference from characters like Nick or Anthony: Andrew still believes things will improve on their own. He hasn't accepted limitation yet. That unbroken expectation is both his most human quality and his greatest vulnerability.

---

## Sexuality & Desire

Andrew is bisexual but **inexperienced and uncertain**.

* Attracted to both men and women
* More drawn to masculine confidence than any specific gender
* Limited dating experience (one or two brief attempts, nothing sustained)
* Watches queer spaces from a distance without entering them
* Desires **strength** and **presence** in partners
* Struggles to imagine anyone desiring him in return

**What He Wants:**
Andrew doesn't just want to be *with* someone powerful — he wants to **become** someone who could stand beside them without shrinking.

---

## Intelligence — The Double-Edged Quality

Andrew is genuinely smart, and this matters more than it initially appears.

* Picks up on social dynamics quickly
* Understands systems and hierarchies instinctively
* Thinks analytically under pressure
* Learns fast when motivated

This intelligence is currently **unfocused** — pointed inward at self-criticism rather than outward at the world. But it is real, and Butch Bear notices it.

Where other targets offer Butch Bear obedience, Andrew offers something rarer: **the capacity to lead**.

---

## Vulnerability to Corruption

Andrew is **precisely positioned** for Butch Bear's coercive methodology.

Why he's susceptible:
* Young and uncertain
* Desperate for maturity and presence
* Isolated from strong support networks
* Eager to please authority figures
* Willing to accept certainty over autonomy
* Craves shortcuts to becoming "finished"
* Smart enough to be useful — and to be specifically targeted because of it
