# Mike — Character Sheet

## Basic Information
- **Name:** Mike
- **Age:** Early 30s
- **Sexuality:** Gay
- **Location:** Large modern city
- **Occupation:** Barista at a coffee shop chain
- **Life Status:** Emotionally muted, drifting

---

## Appearance
Before his transformation, Mike is physically unremarkable — not unattractive, but easy to overlook.

- **Build:** Slim, average, lightly defined
- **Height:** Average
- **Posture:** Relaxed but withdrawn; shoulders slightly forward
- **Hair:** Short, casually kept, no strong styling
- **Eyes:** Steel grey — cool and quiet, easy to overlook in passing but present when you look directly at him.
- **Facial Hair:** Minimal stubble at most
- **Expression:** Neutral, distant, quietly pleasant
- **Clothing:**
  - Coffee shop uniform or simple basics
  - Jeans, plain shirts, practical shoes
  - Clothes chosen for comfort and convenience rather than self-expression

**Overall Impression:**  
Mike looks like someone who blends into the rhythm of the city. Familiar, but rarely noticed. The kind of person people recognize only after seeing him every day.

---

## Personality
- Calm and soft-spoken
- Emotionally muted rather than insecure
- Kind, but passive
- Observant, often lost in thought
- Low sense of urgency

Mike isn’t unhappy, and he isn’t hopeful either. He exists in a comfortable middle ground where nothing feels *wrong enough* to change.

---

## Inner Life
Mike experiences life at a low volume.

- Rarely feels strong joy or deep sadness
- Lets days pass without marking them
- Has desires, but they remain vague and unformed
- Feels like life is happening *around* him rather than *to* him

He doesn’t resent this state — he simply accepts it.

---

## Relationship to His Body
Mike’s relationship with his body is neutral.

- Uses it, but doesn’t inhabit it
- No strong body image issues
- Doesn’t linger in mirrors or photos
- Treats his body as a tool for getting through the day

His body is something he lives *inside*, not something he actively experiences.

---

## Work & Routine
- **Job:** Barista at a large coffee shop chain
- **Skill Level:** Competent, reliable, unremarkable

Mike enjoys coffee itself:
- Likes learning about roasts and origins
- Curious about new flavors
- Willing to try unusual blends
- Enjoys recommending drinks to customers

However, the job is not his calling. It provides structure, not meaning.

Coffee is the interest.
The job is just the container.

---

## Social Life
- Friendly with coworkers, but not close
- Limited social circle
- Few strong connections
- Rarely initiates plans

People like Mike. They just don’t know him well.

---

## Core Wound
Mike’s defining issue is **absence**, not pain.

He never chose his life — it simply happened.

- No strong regrets
- No strong ambitions
- No clear sense of purpose

He floats through routines, trusting that something will eventually make him feel *awake*.

---

## Catalyst Potential
Mike’s openness is his most important trait.

He is:
- Curious without skepticism
- Willing to try new things
- Open to experience without expectation

That openness is what allows him to brew and drink the strange coffee that appears on his doorstep.

He doesn’t believe it will change his life.

He just wants to know what it tastes like.

---

## Core Themes
- Emotional quiet
- Drifting vs. choosing
- Untapped potential
- Life before embodiment
- Waiting without knowing what for

---

*This character sheet defines Mike strictly **before** his transformation into Superbear.*
