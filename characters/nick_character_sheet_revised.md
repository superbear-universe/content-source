# Nick — Character Sheet (Revised)

## Basic Information
- **Name:** Nick  
- **Age:** Early 30s  
- **Sexuality:** Gay  
- **Location:** Small apartment in a large city  
- **Life Status:** Financially stable, emotionally paused

---

## Appearance (Before Transformation)
Nick is of **average height** with a **soft, lightly built frame** that suggests potential rather than absence. His body is not fragile or sharply lean; instead, it carries a quiet fullness that hasn’t yet been claimed.

- **Racial / Ethnic Background:** White North-American, Scandinavian descent  
- **Skin Tone:** Fair / light  

- **Build:**  
  Slim-to-average with gentle softness. Narrower shoulders, a lightly filled chest, and lean limbs that feel unresolved rather than lacking. His body reads as restrained, not weak.

- **Posture:**  
  Slightly guarded but intact. He often stands with his weight shifted, hands in pockets, occupying less space than he’s entitled to. When relaxed, his posture briefly hints at groundedness and quiet strength.

- **Hair:**  
  Blonde, medium length, naturally wavy. Casually messy, falling into place without effort. The same hair that will later suit Supercub perfectly, simply fuller and more confident.

- **Face & Features:**  
  A warm, open face with soft lines. Gentle jaw, subtly rounded cheeks, and an approachable expression. His features already read as emotionally safe.

- **Eyes & Expression:**  
  Clear, bright blue — warm rather than cold, expressive and present, often carrying thoughtfulness and empathy. He makes eye contact more than he realizes, especially when listening.

- **Facial Hair:**  
  Clean-shaven or light stubble by habit, not preference. He doesn’t think of himself as someone who could “pull off” a beard.

- **Body Hair:**  
  Minimal but present — faint hair on arms and legs, subtle signs of latent beary traits.

- **Glasses:**  
  Rounded, well-fitted frames that complement his face and emphasize his eyes.

- **Clothing:**  
  - A familiar grey hoodie worn often  
  - White or neutral t-shirts  
  - Well-worn jeans  
  - Clean sneakers  

  His clothes are casual and comfortable. They don’t hide him — they simply don’t celebrate him yet.

**Overall Impression:**  
Nick looks kind, approachable, and quietly present. He doesn’t project strength yet, but he no longer reads as invisible. It’s easy to imagine this same man becoming Supercub without contradiction.

---

## Personality
Nick is gentle, attentive, and morally sensitive.

- Thoughtful and introspective  
- Empathetic to a fault  
- Conflict-avoidant, especially in public or professional settings  
- Hesitant to assert himself under pressure  
- Quietly principled

Nick does not doubt his values — he doubts his *right* to insist on them. His hesitation comes from restraint, not lack of conviction.

---

## Work & Routine
- **Job:** Office worker reviewing insurance claims  
- **Original Motivation:** Wanted to help people  
- **Reality:** Spends his days finding technical reasons to deny aid

Nick dislikes his job not because it’s dull, but because it contradicts his sense of care. The work represents stability without meaning — safety without alignment.

His coworkers appear untroubled by the ethical implications, which deepens his sense of quiet separation.

---

## Core Wound
Nick’s defining wound is **deferred becoming**.

He did everything that was expected of him:
- Went to college  
- Got a degree  
- Took a stable job

But he never felt invited to fully *arrive* in his life.

It doesn’t feel too late.
It feels **paused**.

Nick isn’t grieving a missed chance — he’s waiting for permission to begin.

---

## Relationship to His Body
Nick’s relationship with his body is defined by **distance**, not dislike.

He doesn’t hate how he looks. He simply doesn’t feel fully present inside himself.

- He rarely lingers in mirrors, but doesn’t avoid them  
- Physical sensations register at low volume  
- His body feels functional rather than expressive

On some level, Nick knows his body could be more.

- He notices his shoulders sometimes  
- Feels aware of unresolved weight and softness  
- Senses potential without urgency

He once tried the gym, learned the routines, and then stopped — not from failure, but from lack of meaning. He didn’t know *why* he was there.

Nick’s body is not rejected or neglected.

It is **waiting**.

---

## Desire & Fantasy
Nick longs for change, purpose, and embodiment.

He is drawn to muscular, beary, physically confident men — not only with attraction, but recognition. Their bodies signal permission, groundedness, and presence.

What he wants is not dominance.

It’s the ease with which they seem to inhabit themselves.

Nick doesn’t just want to be *with* someone like that.

He wants to **become** someone like that.

---

## Catalyst: Encounter with Superbear
Nick’s routine life is disrupted when he is attacked during a street robbery and rescued by the superhero **Superbear**.

What affects Nick most isn’t the violence — it’s the care.

Superbear treats him with warmth, attention, and calm strength. For the first time, someone powerful sees Nick not as invisible or weak, but as worth protecting.

This moment plants the seed of transformation.

---

## Core Themes
- Embodiment as permission  
- Gentle masculinity  
- Moral courage vs. comfort  
- Desire as a guide, not a flaw  
- Becoming visible by arriving fully
