# Andrea "Andrew" Ferretti — Character Sheet

### *From Exchange Student to Brotherhood Goon to Super Orso*

*Bearing Universe — Main Timeline*

---

## Overview

Andrew Ferretti is a twenty-year-old Italian exchange student who becomes, in sequence: a target, a Brotherhood Goon, a freed captive, and a hero. His arc is not about destiny. It is about a specific vulnerability being exploited, the slow work of undoing that exploitation, and a single moment of protective fury that changes everything.

He does not become a hero because he was chosen. He becomes a hero because he chose.

---

## State One — The Exchange Student

### Basic Information

- **Full Name:** Andrea Ferretti
- **Known As:** Andrew
- **Age:** 20
- **Nationality:** Italian — from Bologna
- **Location:** Large modern city, university district
- **Sexuality:** Bisexual
- **Status:** Exchange student, international law and political science

### Appearance

Andrew looks clean, healthy, and conventionally attractive — in a way that feels understated rather than claimed. There is nothing wrong with how he looks. There is also nothing that insists.

- **Build:** Lean, lightly athletic — slim frame with subtle muscle tone, no mass or thickness
- **Height:** 1.84m / 6'0" — carries it without presence
- **Hair:** Thick, dark brown — short on the sides, natural volume on top, casually tidy
- **Eyes:** Warm brown — steady but slightly reserved
- **Facial Hair:** Clean-shaven — smooth jawline, no visible growth
- **Body Hair:** Light on arms and legs, chest mostly smooth
- **Skin:** Clear, lightly warm-toned Mediterranean complexion — faint freckles visible up close
- **Posture:** Neutral, faintly guarded — shoulders relaxed but slightly rounded inward

His body does not look unfinished in the sense of immaturity. It looks unfinished in the sense of potential not yet claimed.

### Personality

- Polite to the point of self-erasure
- Analytically sharp — picks up on social dynamics faster than most
- Conflict-avoidant, especially with authority
- Intellectually curious and genuinely intelligent
- Observant — notices more than he lets on, acts on none of it
- Eager to please, sometimes to his detriment

Andrew is the kind of person who apologizes when someone else bumps into him. He also notices the dynamics in a room faster than almost anyone. He just doesn't know what to do with that perception yet.

### Vulnerability

Andrew is precisely positioned for Butch Bear's methodology. Young and uncertain. Financially precarious. Isolated by cultural displacement. Desperate for maturity and physical presence. Willing to accept certainty over autonomy. Smart enough to be specifically useful — and therefore specifically targeted.

Where other targets offer obedience, Andrew offers something rarer: the capacity to lead. Butch Bear notices this. It is why Andrew is selected. It is also, eventually, why Andrew cannot be held.

## State Two — Brotherhood Goon

### How It Happens

Andrew responds to a campus ad for a paid cognition study. The address takes him to the dockyards. The man who answers the door is Bill. By the time Andrew understands what is happening, the door is locked behind him.

Bill uses cigar smoke — concentrated corrupted bearing energy — forcing Andrew to inhale it against his will. The transformation is not gradual. It is fast, total, and experienced by Andrew as something being done to him rather than something he is becoming. He fights it. He loses.

The harness goes on. The last buckle clicks shut. Andrew straightens, looks at Bill, and waits. In his pocket, his phone is buzzing. No one answers it.

### Appearance as a Goon

The transformation produces a body Andrew spent years wanting and would never have chosen this way.

- **Build:** Massively muscular, thick-set, physically imposing throughout
- **Height:** Grown slightly from 1.84m
- **Hair:** Black, unchanged
- **Eyes:** Dark brown — present but blank, the expression behind them suppressed
- **Beard:** Full, dense, black — arrived in seconds during transformation
- **Body Hair:** Dense across chest, stomach, arms — complete contrast to before
- **Skin:** Same Mediterranean complexion, now carrying the settled quality of a completed transformation
- **Posture:** Shoulders back, chest open, weight distributed with automatic confidence — not his posture, but the body's

He looks, from the outside, like a man who chose this. He did not.

### Attire

Standard Brotherhood Goon uniform:

- Sleeveless black shirt
- Black leather harness — thick straps, heavy buckles, worn against bare skin
- Leather pants and boots

The harness is not decorative. It is the conduit for Bill's corrupted bearing energy — the mechanism that keeps Andrew's autonomy suppressed rather than merely damaged.

### Consciousness as a Goon

Andrew is a forced Goon, which means the suppression is never total. His autonomy is suppressed, not dissolved. The original Andrew registers fragments throughout — moments of recognition that arrive and dissolve before they can mean anything. He is present in the way someone is present during a nightmare: aware, unable to act.

During the fight with Superbear, these fragments surface more frequently. Something about the cape. The bear crest. *Superbear.* The thought arrives, dissolves, returns.

He knows, somewhere underneath, exactly what he is doing. He cannot stop it. That knowledge is the thing that will matter most when the harness breaks.

### The Cigar Smoke Power

Because Andrew's transformation was performed through direct, intense exposure to Bill's cigar smoke rather than standard Brotherhood methods, he carries more concentrated corrupted bearing energy than a typical Goon. In the field this manifests as temporary enhanced strength — enough to make him a credible physical match for Superbear at close range. It is not sustainable. It burns through him. But it lasts long enough to make the fight real.

## State Three — Recovery

### The Break

Superbear defeats Andrew in the field. The impact is enough to snap one strap of the harness. The gap is immediate — rainbow energy from Superbear's strike bleeds through the opening. The suppression does not lift gradually. It breaks all at once.

Andrew is suddenly, fully present in his own body. Enormous, bearded, hairy, kneeling in a ruined harness, facing the hero he was just trying to hurt. The horror arrives fast. He speaks in Italian first.

### Physical Regression

Removed from the harness and taken somewhere safe, Andrew's body begins to reverse within the first day. The mass recedes. The body hair thins. The beard softens and shortens. By the end of the second day he looks almost exactly like the exchange student who answered a campus ad.

He watches it happen in a small mirror. Relief and loss arrive simultaneously. Neither one is clean.

### The Recovery Period

Andrew is kept in a guest bedroom. The corruption without the harness as a stable conduit is unstable — it resurfaces in episodes that require restraint. He is tied to the bed during the worst of it. He understands why and does not fight it.

During lucid periods he is sharp, cooperative, and useful. He has been inside Bill's operation. He knows the recruitment method, the warehouse, the harness mechanics, the cigar smoke process. He volunteers this information because dismantling the Brotherhood is the only productive thing available to him from a guest bedroom.

During corruption episodes he is not himself. He surfaces afterward knowing what happened, unable to stop it from happening again. The knowledge is worse than amnesia would be.

The healing process — Superbear and Supercub pushing consent-based bearing energy through him incrementally — is painful and slow. It feels like something fundamentally at odds with what is still in him. It does not feel like transformation. It feels like medicine. He learns to tell the difference between what Bill put in him and what the heroes are putting in him. The distinction is absolute.

### The Realization

Alone one evening, Andrew's analytical mind does what it has always done — runs the room, connects the data. He arrives at it quietly: the campus ad, the financial pressure, the isolation, the body insecurities, the desperate need to become something. Every vulnerability precisely targeted. He was not found by accident. He was selected because of exactly who he was.

The disgust that follows is cold and clarifying. He does not tell Superbear or Supercub that night. He lies in the dark with it and lets it become something harder than shame.

## State Four — Super Orso

### The Transformation

Andrew is nearly recovered — well enough to take a supervised walk outside, to feel the city again, to notice that he is almost himself. He sees it happening across the street: a Brotherhood Goon working a young man, isolated and uncertain, the body language of someone who expects to be overlooked. Andrew recognizes every beat of it. He recognizes the target because he was the target.

The fury arrives before the thought does.

He moves toward them. No plan, no calculation — pure protective instinct overriding everything else. The healing energy that Superbear and Supercub spent weeks putting into him finds the moment it was waiting for. It ignites.

The surge is fast and total. Mass returning. Body hair spreading. The beard arriving all at once. The body expanding past what the Brotherhood made and arriving somewhere new — somewhere that was never Bill's. Blue light. The suit materializing around him.

Super Orso, standing in a city street, slightly stunned by his own existence.

The Brotherhood Goon takes one look and runs. Andrew checks on the young man. Says something in Italian by accident.

### Why This Transformation Is Different

The Brotherhood body was imposed. This one is arrived at. The distinction is not cosmetic — the Super Orso form is the expression of weeks of consent-based bearing energy finding its release point through a genuine act of will. Superbear and Supercub did not make him a hero. They gave him the capacity. He supplied the moment.

The suit does not look like Brotherhood attire. It does not look like anything Bill made. It looks like it belongs to Italy.

### Appearance as Super Orso

Every Mediterranean feature the original Andrew carried is preserved and enhanced. He does not read as generically bear. He reads as specifically, unmistakably Italian.

- **Build:** Heavily muscular, thick-set, powerfully built throughout — broad shoulders, dense chest, substantial mass in arms and thighs
- **Height:** 1.90m / 6'3"
- **Hair:** Black, thick, naturally voluminous — unchanged
- **Eyes:** Dark brown — warm, steady, carrying full presence
- **Beard:** Full, dense, dark — frames his face with authority
- **Body Hair:** Dense across chest, stomach, arms
- **Skin:** Deep olive Mediterranean complexion, settled and lived-in
- **Posture:** Open, grounded, occupies space without apology

His face retains the symmetry and defined cheekbones of the original Andrew. Nothing about it reads as youthful anymore. He looks like a man who has finished waiting.

### Costume

The suit is a direct visual homage to the Azzurri — the blue of Italy's national sports teams. It is not a costume. It is civic identity worn as a uniform.

- **Primary Color:** Azzurri blue
- **Accents:** White trim at collar, cuffs, and boots
- **Cape:** Deep red — evokes the Italian tricolore without literalism
- **Chest Emblem:** Stylized bear head on a shield crest — heraldic in proportion, modern in execution
- **Wristbands:** Blue leather
- **Boots:** Blue, mid-calf — built for ground-level mobility

### Powers & Abilities

**Superhuman Strength and Durability** — The physical foundation. Expressed with control and precision. He hits harder when he has had time to read the situation.

**Ground-Level Mobility** — Explosive sprinting and leaping. Closes distance faster than opponents can track. No flight. He does not need to survey the city from above — he runs across it. In practice: rooftops, terracotta tiles, the compressed geography of Italian cities. He moves like something between a bear and a leopard.

**Blue Construct Generation** — Projects holographic blue energy constructs: shields, swords, maces, any form the situation demands. They appear as geometric blue light — architectural, almost decorative. They hit like iron. Opponents consistently underestimate them on first encounter.

His default forms draw from Roman and Italian historical warfare — scutum shields, gladii, war maces. This is not a limitation. It is a vocabulary. The shapes his mind reaches for first.

### Fighting Style

Gladiator — in the strategic sense. He reads his opponent before committing. Uses the environment. Chooses the moment. Then acts without hesitation.

He is not a brawler. He is a thinker who fights at close range. His mobility closes the distance before opponents have processed the movement. His constructs adapt in real time. He wins by being two decisions ahead.

He is most dangerous when he has had thirty seconds to observe.

### Personality as Super Orso

Super Orso is the version of Andrew who stopped deferring. The original Andrew lived at muted volume — present, intelligent, unacted upon. Super Orso acts. That quality of claimed, inhabited presence is not performed. It is simply the default.

The analytical mind is unchanged and sharpened. The politeness is gone — replaced not by rudeness but by directness. He no longer apologizes for taking up space because the thought no longer occurs to him.

He is measured rather than warm. Earns trust through consistency rather than charm. Dry humor, rarely deployed, lands hard when it appears. His Italian identity is lived rather than performed — he is specifically, recognizably from somewhere, and that specificity is part of what makes him a different kind of hero than Mike or Nick.

The desire to be legible — to be read as a man, to belong in adult spaces — has been answered so completely that he no longer thinks about it. That particular hunger is gone.

## Relationships

**Superbear** — The field encounter that freed him. Superbear takes Andrew in, coordinates the healing, and is present throughout the recovery. Andrew does not know his civilian identity. The relationship is warm but carries the specific weight of debt that has not yet been fully processed. Mike is the reason Super Orso exists. That does not make them teammates — not yet.

**Supercub** — Present throughout the recovery. Nick's curiosity about the Brotherhood gives the convalescence purpose — Andrew is the most useful intelligence source on Bill's operation they have. Nick is the more cautious of the two heroes regarding trust, and Andrew respects that caution as honest. Their dynamic is direct in a way Mike and Andrew's isn't.

**Butch Bear / Bill** — Andrew was Bill's asset and Bill was Andrew's captor. The disgust Andrew carries toward Bill is not rage — it is colder than that. Bill targeted who Andrew was. Super Orso intends to make that a mistake Bill does not recover from.

**Salvatore Bruno** — The Brotherhood's Italian capo, based in Naples. Andrew does not know Bruno exists yet. Bruno does not know Andrew exists yet. That will change.

**His Family** — His mother calls. His father calls. His grandmother calls separately. He is twenty years old, supposedly on an exchange program in a foreign city, and he now looks like he ate the previous version of himself. He has not found a version of the video call home that works. He is still trying.

---

## Base of Operations

Super Orso operates in Italy. He returns home after the recovery period — the exchange program ends, and the man who returns to Bologna is not the man who left. Italy is not backdrop. It is the point. His home country has its own threats. It needs its own hero.

The Azzurri suit is not costuming. It is a statement of belonging.

## Notes

The leopard quality of his rooftop mobility is noted and intentional. He is a bear who moves like a cat across Italian rooftops. The contrast is more interesting than consistency.

His hero name — Super Orso — is definitive. *Orso* is Italian for bear. The name carries national identity without requiring translation to do its work.

The Romulus and Remus mythology uses a wolf rather than a bear. This direction was considered and set aside.
