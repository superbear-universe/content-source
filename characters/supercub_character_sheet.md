# Supercub — Character Sheet

## Basic Information
- **Hero Name:** Supercub  
- **Civilian Identity:** Nick  
- **Age:** Early 30s  
- **Sexuality:** Gay  
- **Role:** Superhero sidekick  
- **Primary Affiliation:** Superbear  
- **Public Status:** Known ally and secondary protector  
- **Symbolic Theme:** Support, embodiment, quiet strength  

---

## Appearance (After Transformation)
Supercub is massively built and unmistakably powerful, yet his presence reads as **reassuring rather than commanding**. Where Superbear dominates a space naturally, Supercub **anchors** it.

- **Build:** Thick-set and muscular; broad shoulders, powerful arms, strong legs, and a solid, muscular gut  
- **Posture:** Open and grounded; slightly less expansive than Superbear, but confident and steady  
- **Face:** Full beard framing a warm, expressive face
- **Eyes:** Clear, bright blue — warm rather than cold, expressive and present
- **Hair:** Short to medium length, naturally styled, slightly tousled  
- **Expression:** Friendly, gentle, often smiling — with a trace of shyness beneath the confidence  

**Overall Impression:**  
Supercub looks like someone you want standing beside you in a crisis. He is visibly strong, comfortably physical, and emotionally present — a hero whose power feels safe to lean against.

---

## Super Suit
- **Primary Colors:** Coordinated with Superbear’s palette, but slightly lighter or softer in contrast  
- **Accents:** Gold or warm-toned detailing  
- **Emblem:** Bear-themed insignia distinct from Superbear’s, signaling junior status without diminishing identity  
- **Cape:** Present, but shorter or less dramatic than Superbear’s  
- **Fit:** Skin-tight and contoured, emphasizing mass, thickness, and physical reassurance  

The suit reinforces that Supercub is **not a replacement**, but a complement — designed for backup, presence, and shared action.

---

## Personality (As Supercub)
- Gentle, attentive, and empathetic  
- Confident, but still discovering himself  
- Less comfortable in the spotlight  
- Earnest and emotionally open  
- Slightly timid compared to Superbear, but deeply sincere  
- Often responds to Superbear’s humor with dry wit, fond eye-rolling, and quiet amusement


Supercub smiles easily and laughs often, but he doesn’t perform confidence — he grows into it moment by moment. When interacting with civilians, he instinctively softens his voice, lowers himself slightly, or leans in to meet them at eye level.

---

## Powers & Abilities
- **Superhuman Strength:** Immense physical power, expressed carefully and deliberately  
- **Flight:** Controlled and buoyant, often accompanied by subtle rainbow energy trails  
- **Enhanced Durability:** Highly resistant to harm and exhaustion  
- **Endurance:** Can sustain long periods of physical effort  
- **Calming Presence:** People feel safer and steadier around him  

In action, Supercub’s power feels **joyful and abundant**, never aggressive. His strength supports rather than overwhelms.

---

## Combat & Field Role
Supercub is built to **back people up**.

- Holds lines while others move  
- Catches falling civilians or debris  
- Provides physical support and reassurance  
- Excels in rescue, containment, and protection  

He is strongest when paired with Superbear — not as a shadow, but as a stabilizing counterweight.

---

## Relationship to His Body
Supercub is still learning to fully inhabit his new form.

- Takes pleasure in his size and strength  
- Touches his arms, chest, or gut unconsciously when grounding himself  
- Occasionally surprised by how much space he occupies  
- Experiences embodiment as something *new but welcome*  

His body represents a dream realized — though he’s still adjusting to believing it’s real.

---

## Relationship to Superbear
Supercub deeply respects Superbear.

- Sees him as a mentor, and inspiration  
- Trusts his judgment instinctively  
- Feels safest and most confident fighting at his side  

Supercub’s association with Superbear is often described publicly as a “sidekick” role.
In practice, this reflects experience and visibility rather than hierarchy.

Supercub is best understood as a **superhero in training**, operating by choice within a partnership defined by equality and mutual respect.

Superbear does not command Supercub.
Supercub does not obey Superbear.

They function as a team, with leadership emerging contextually rather than structurally.


---

## Relationship to His Past Self (Nick)
Supercub carries Nick with him.

Nick’s empathy, moral sensitivity, and longing to help others remain fully intact — now housed in a body capable of acting on them. Where Nick once hesitated, Supercub steps forward.

He is not a rejection of who Nick was.  
He is Nick, **finally allowed to take up space**.

---

## Motivation & Values
Supercub acts because:
- People need help  
- He can help  
- He remembers what it felt like not to feel strong  

He believes strength should be shared, protection should be gentle, and that being visible can give others permission to exist more fully.

---

## Public Perception
- Seen as kind, solid, and approachable  
- Trusted quickly by civilians  
- Viewed as Superbear’s reliable right hand  
- Often described as “the nice one” — and wears that proudly  

Children wave. Adults relax. People feel steadier when he’s nearby.

---

## Core Themes
- Strength as support  
- Masculinity without dominance  
- Embodiment as healing  
- Power expressed through care  
- Learning to take up space  

---

*This character sheet defines Supercub strictly **after** Nick’s transformation into a superhero.*  
*Transformation details are intentionally omitted.*
