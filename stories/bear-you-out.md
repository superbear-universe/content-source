Chris was having a great night. Music thumped through the walls of the bar, lights splashed color across the dance floor, and laughter filled the air. He’d just ordered another drink when his phone buzzed again.

*Babe calling.*

He sighed, half-smiling and half-annoyed. He loved his boyfriend, but the timing was terrible. Still, he stepped outside, weaving past the door and into the cool night air.

“Hey,” Chris said into the phone, already straining to hear over the music. “Yeah, I’m outside. It’s loud in there.”

He wandered down the street until he spotted a narrow alley, dimly lit and quiet. Perfect. He leaned against the brick wall, relaxing a little as the noise faded.

That was when he noticed the footsteps.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/537254120d904b65b3bdd0d737487f15/25faae4c3916c285-44/s640x960/a5939a8960baafb51d887073ca02190900be5656.png)

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/456f068ff838ddaa3c495838eaba3ef4/25faae4c3916c285-be/s640x960/19231886ed384cea64c213dab90c34ba312bbd95.png)

Two figures emerged from deeper in the alley, both hooded and moving with deliberate slowness. Chris’s stomach tightened.

“Listen,” one of them said, voice low. “Hand over the phone. Wallet too.”

The glint of metal caught the light. Knives.

Chris froze, heart pounding. His grip tightened around the phone as his mind raced, too fast, too loud, too helpless.

Then…

FLASH.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/79583473bc283ab2f634a8d0056a6091/25faae4c3916c285-a8/s640x960/1300466985272bb83140905dea0d2a50386827c1.png)

The alley exploded with color.

A blinding burst of rainbow light flooded the space, forcing everyone to shield their eyes. When it faded, something massive stood between Chris and the robbers.

A towering figure in blue and red. Broad shoulders. A powerful stance. A cape settling behind him like a curtain.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/a9d3d97f9f815256ac1007be1ce70f57/25faae4c3916c285-5b/s640x960/8ad43badffac37de4e8fb4fb95fa522e485dc357.png)

Superbear.

The robbers recovered first, confusion turning into anger.

“What the hell is this?” one snarled. “Some kind of freak?”

They laughed nervously, tossing out cruel, homophobic slurs and waving their knives like they still had control.

Superbear raised his hands slowly, palms out.

“Hey,” he said calmly. “No one needs to get hurt tonight.”

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/dab2e748d29a361fff21fb2880b8a2fb/25faae4c3916c285-3b/s640x960/fb531a4a18d96d1fdff922c7c18cf96d29336995.png)

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/ed4dd156ed5cbff9d3cc3c7b6b108651/25faae4c3916c285-13/s640x960/754d5412e3b3ca5edd1dbad6689fa4d7b364d5bc.png)

The robbers smirked and stepped closer.

That’s when Superbear’s smile changed.

A mischievous twinkle flashed in his eyes.

Rainbow energy burst from his hands, brilliant and alive, coiling through the air like glowing ropes. In an instant, they wrapped around the robbers’ arms and legs, yanking them off their feet.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/0ee23aade07d56c3d959242004d50d36/25faae4c3916c285-00/s640x960/df56f34ab44b997d728bdbbd3c9e272e860a1fd9.png)

They screamed as they were lifted into the air, dangling helplessly while knives clattered to the ground.

“Let us go!” one begged. “Please!”

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/c4cdb68d78bd11a9bcdd9a50bf58a8b5/25faae4c3916c285-05/s640x960/f298d0f759e5d9afb1f7b18e0b0073532433578f.png)

Superbear chuckled and floated them just a little higher for emphasis.

“Maybe think twice next time.”

With a casual flick of his wrists, he released them. The energy vanished, dropping the men unceremoniously onto the pavement. They didn’t wait. They scrambled to their feet and bolted out of the alley in pure terror.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/e4aa4ffd2b5ebc2d86271173b3c25158/25faae4c3916c285-90/s640x960/e8c4971a8b557b8ba7704a3a936bb9ebcaecce26.png)

Silence returned.

Chris stood there, stunned.

Superbear turned to him immediately, his voice gentle now.

“You okay?”

Chris nodded, his breath shaky. “Yeah… I think so.”

Superbear placed a reassuring hand on his shoulder, grounding him. The sheer size of the hero and the warmth in his presence were overwhelming in the best way.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/e0efcd97199baf6ea0b2fde759b8447f/25faae4c3916c285-c5/s640x960/de06908f89c553064fb33fb7b759ede428f27eef.png)

“I… thank you,” Chris said. “I don’t even know what to say.”

“Anytime,” Superbear replied with a grin.

He offered to walk Chris home, and Chris accepted without hesitation. Together, they strolled down the sidewalk, the city suddenly feeling safer. They talked easily about the bar, about life, about fear and confidence.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/648706f6120baaaac0b1550546f337b7/25faae4c3916c285-97/s640x960/2d6abf86227116304521d96ed25b2a1847b27d91.png)

After a moment, Chris hesitated, then admitted, “I wish I was big. Strong. Like you. So I wouldn’t feel so helpless.”

Superbear stopped.

He studied Chris carefully.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/6733d7cb39ed84917e497829c1e0138e/25faae4c3916c285-af/s640x960/34a3557a5fe6b0d9926e09a7b7064bea0361fcf4.png)

“I can do that,” he said. “But I need you to understand. It’s permanent. You won’t just be stronger. You’ll be a lot more bear.”

Chris didn’t hesitate.

“I’d love nothing more.”

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/68db27ba5c6dd2f3f1d96477565c5bad/25faae4c3916c285-16/s640x960/1b91bfc5788abaa72f856548011a4a1a1c91f1f9.png)

Superbear’s smile widened.

“Alright then,” he said. “Let’s bear you out!”

Superbear’s hand pressed firmly against Chris’s chest.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/33bdcff452c1732e0391d167eb4f1549/25faae4c3916c285-4c/s640x960/be46ae29d4fd20a647e0c8d954ff95606b804847.png)

For a heartbeat, nothing happened.

Then warmth bloomed beneath Superbear’s palm, deep, steady, and powerful, spreading outward like a second pulse layered over Chris’s own heartbeat. Threads of rainbow light slipped between Superbear’s fingers and sank into Chris’s body, glowing beneath his skin.

Chris inhaled sharply.

The air around him began to hum.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/f8071ee51347c2491e1476714fe00849/25faae4c3916c285-69/s640x960/d41ba934b10448e5d2f1778243876cec81e72709.png)

The energy surged through his shoulders first. His posture straightened involuntarily, spine lengthening as if gravity itself had loosened its grip. A strange pressure built in his arms, not pain but expansion, his sleeves tightening as muscle swelled beneath them. He looked down in disbelief as his forearms thickened, veins becoming more pronounced, strength gathering where there had once been uncertainty.

His chest followed.

Chris felt his ribcage broaden, his breath deepening as his torso grew heavier and more solid. The fabric of his shirt pulled tight, seams stretching as his chest expanded outward, his shoulders rolling back naturally and confidently. He laughed, half shocked and half exhilarated, as he felt weight settle into his frame. Not burden. Presence.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/7e606575592a2fe256d6fa2f2c19cd0d/25faae4c3916c285-bf/s640x960/e8ced111781a91e2afe3c6687902ce4e63f0ef8c.png)

Then came the warmth across his stomach.

It spread slower there, deliberate, reshaping rather than sharpening, building a powerful core with mass and gravity. His midsection pushed outward, firm and heavy, anchoring him. The sensation was grounding, like finally standing firmly in his own space instead of shrinking away from it.

Hair followed.

Chris gasped as heat rippled across his skin, across his arms, chest, and stomach, darkening, thickening, filling in. A deep, natural growth that felt ancient and right, like something long overdue. His jaw tingled intensely as his beard surged fuller and longer, framing his face and changing his reflection entirely.

The light intensified.

Chris’s boots lifted off the pavement.

He floated upward, arms drifting out to his sides as the transformation continued through his legs. Thighs thickened, calves strengthened, and his jeans pulled taut as his lower body gained the same solid power as the rest of him. Fabric strained and tore in places, unable to keep up with what he was becoming.

Chris tipped his head back, eyes closed, laughter breaking free as the energy reached its peak. He wasn’t afraid. He wasn’t overwhelmed.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/8454b1f47ca895542952e44dbfffdb40/25faae4c3916c285-c0/s640x960/75c8af19278250c61398663f63ea3868bccb0409.png)

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/8973855384f83ce24bdc25150960d448/25faae4c3916c285-49/s640x960/0f306d81c8aa78e83a8d2e990de6cfbf09dfefa7.png)

He felt strong.

He felt seen.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/9584c3a9cac12ef5463d4902f96e1a25/25faae4c3916c285-6f/s640x960/41b61c788f1e662ac659300eb61eb5849e875d1b.png)

The rainbow light flared brilliantly, then softened.

Gently, almost reverently, it faded away.

Chris descended, boots touching the ground with a new sense of weight and balance. When he opened his eyes, the alley felt different. Not smaller, but more manageable. Like it finally fit him.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/b6a819ca1f9f9dcbbeffd92b3a0b6fbb/25faae4c3916c285-f3/s640x960/a02580e7543351c23ad5abd5f04c4f449fdce49f.png)

He looked down at himself in disbelief. Broad shoulders. Thick arms. A solid, burly frame wrapped in tattered clothes that no longer defined him.

Superbear stood nearby, arms crossed, smiling with unmistakable pride.

“How do you feel?” he asked.

Chris flexed his hands slowly, feeling power respond effortlessly.  
“Like I finally take up the space I was always meant to.”

Superbear nodded.  
“Knew it.”

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/cd0ae1d61a1b69e99337982742cbae81/25faae4c3916c285-70/s640x960/69ec439d1cb9265b10a8122b8226a6d4ba8302f1.png)

Chris laughed, joy bubbling over. “Thank you. For everything.”

They said their goodbyes, and with a powerful leap, Superbear shot into the night sky, disappearing among the stars.

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/26159a7ef01eedaeb2a29fc2ecaac9eb/25faae4c3916c285-4e/s640x960/496ccda217961340ad0736e0910279f3d98e8321.png)

Chris stood there for a moment, then picked up his phone again.

His boyfriend answered immediately.

“You are not going to believe what just happened,” Chris said, grinning wider than he ever had before.

And for the first time in a long while, he felt truly unstoppable.
